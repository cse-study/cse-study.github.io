ICLR2021에서 oral로 발표된 Yang song 저자의 “Score-based generative modeling through stochastic differential equations.“를 리뷰합니다.

Summary

Background

  • “Generative Modeling by Estimating Gradients of the Data Distribution” 논문에서 제시한 방법을 Noise Conditional Score Network, NCSN라 부름
  • Proposed method는, NCSN에서 noise scale의 수를 inifinite으로 일반화하여 exact log-likelihood computation, and controllable generation for inverse problem solving.
  • Stochastic Differential Equations:

Proposed method

  1. Perturbing data with an SDE
  2. Reversing the SDE for sample generation
  3. Estimating the reverse SDE with score-based models and score matching
  4. Probability flow ODE
  5. Controllable generation for inverse problem solving
